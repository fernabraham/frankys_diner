Kids Parties

Themed Grease 50’s style parties

We have a 20 seater table dedicated to kids. Face painters available on request. One of our dedicated waiters will be at your service throughout the party ensuring all your needs are met. Order from our delicious and affordable Kiddies menu.


Kids can make use of the FREE play area!


Baby changing facilities are available if needed.

The Chubby Burger

Franky’s Diner introduces THE CHUBBY BURGER! The challenge is on – If you can eat this gargantuan burger (6 Franky’s beef burger patties stacked on a sesame bun with cheese, jalapeno peppers, bacon, lettuce, tomato, fried onions, dill pickles, mayo, fries, onion rings and coleslaw) in 10 minutes or less you get it for FREE.


You also get your name inscribed on our wall of fame and a T-shirt to prove it.
