Authentic 50's Diner

The Original 50’s Style Diner

Franky’s Diner is an authentic 50’s style diner in the heart of Sea Point open 24 HOURS A DAY! Serving up delicious Hamburgers, Footlongs, Sloppy Joes, curly fries & shakes, this diner couldn’t be more 50’s if it tried. Customers can relax and enjoy some hound-dog music while sitting on the comfy red vinyl stools at the counter or they can choose to snuggle up in the back booth. Franky’s Diner is certainly a trip back in time to an era long gone but never forgotten.
